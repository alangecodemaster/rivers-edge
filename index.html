Our Plans for the Center

We are more than just a recreation center - we are a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting health, wellness, and community engagement. We are building a state-of-the-art facility designed to cater to individuals and families of all ages, providing a wide range of activities and amenities that will keep you entertained, active, and connected. Importantly, it is indoors and serves the Southern Idaho area year-round. Here's what you can expect:

Non-Profit Organization

We are a non-profit organization, meaning your support for our facility will not raise taxes or be run by government entities.

Indoor 25 Yard, 8-Lane Lap Pool

Meant for indoor competitions with stadium seating as well as general exercise for the public.

Separate Recreational Pool

Our recreational pool offers a fun and safe environment for families and individuals to enjoy water activities and socialization.

Hot Tub

Available for therapeutics and general relaxation.

Indoor Kids Splash Zone

Watch your little ones splash and play in our specially designed indoor splash zone, complete with water features and interactive elements.

Indoor Child Play Structures

Let your kids explore and have fun even during the winter with our indoor play structures.

Children's Museum

We are partnering with the Burley Children's Museum to bring the community a creative area for children, complete with play structures and rotating exhibits.

Climbing/Bouldering

Our climbing area will feature walls and bouldering routes suitable for climbers of all skill levels.

Indoor Walking/Running Path

Enjoy our indoor walking/running path, accessible year-round and available for all ages.

Pickelball Courts

Join in on the exciting and fast-paced action of pickleball.

Multipurpose/Conference Rooms

Several upper-level rooms available to host meetings, conferences, or events with flexible seating arrangements.

Outdoor Picnic Area

The recreatoion center is scenically placed to enjoy picnics surrounded by lush greenery and serene ambiance.

What We Are Doing

Extending Recreation in Sourthern Idaho

We proud to serve the communities of Cassia, Minidoka, and the greater Southern Idaho area. River's Edge Recreational Center is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive space for residents of all backgrounds to come together and enjoy a wide range of recreational activities. The recreation center, pools, and other ammenities will be available year-round in our indoor facility so families and individuals can enjoy swimming, exercise, and child play spaces even during the cold winter months.

Our Goals

Why We Are Building a Recreation Center

Community
Needs

Our community and greater surrounding areas desparately need a year-round pool for recreation and competition. Southern Idaho residents have been begging for more indoor recreational spaces. Our rec center will accommodate our community's desires and provide year-round, indoor recreation for everyone.

Recreation for Children & Families

There is not very much for children and families to do in our area during the colder, winter months. Our rec center features indoor play structures, a children's museum, and indoor water play space year-round to provide kids in our community with sustainable recreation.

Stengthening Community

We anticipate River's Edge to attract thousands of people every year, inside and outside our immediate communities. This will have a ripple effect that will bring in more business for local vendors and improve the quality of life for everyone who uses it.
